About ANSUD

With over 14 years of hands-on experience in China's shipping agency sector, ANSUD Shipping Agency Co., Ltd. is a dedicated partner for ship owners and ship managers worldwide.

We specialize in Owner's Husbandry, Protective Agency, Full Port Agency, and specialized services such as dry docking, S&P handover, and newbuilding agency across all major Chinese ports.

Our mission is simple: to safeguard your vessel, crew, cargo, and interests with local expertise, uncompromising standards, and real-time responsiveness. Whether at berth, anchorage, or shipyard, ANSUD ensures your port calls are efficient, cost-effective, and trouble-free.

Why Choose ANSUD?

  • Proven Track Record — 14+ years supporting international ship owners from Asia, Europe, Middle East, and beyond.
  • Nationwide Coverage — On-ground agents in every key Chinese port, with 24/7 availability.
  • Dedicated to Owners — We act as your independent eyes and hands in China, prioritizing your interests above all.
  • Cost Control & Transparency — Competitive fees, detailed disbursement accounts, and no hidden charges.
  • Strong Local Networks — Established relationships with MSA, Customs, Immigration, Ports Authorities, Shipyards, and service providers.
  • Safety & Compliance First — Full adherence to international standards (ISM, ISPS, PSC readiness support).

Core Services

We provide comprehensive Chinese port agency services to meet all your needs

1. Owner's Husbandry Agency

As your dedicated husbandry agent, we provide comprehensive, round-the-clock care for your vessel, crew, and operations in port, at anchorage, or during repairs.

  • Crew change & sign-on/off coordination (including Urgent Visa On Arrival)
  • PSC rectification & assistance
  • Medical support & crew to doctor/hospital
  • Personnel attendance & joining
  • Customs clearance & delivery of spares/parts
  • Cash to Master
  • Courier & stores delivery on board
  • Warehousing & storage facilities
  • SSCEC renewal
  • MSA declaration & liaison
  • Sludge/garbage removal & disposal
  • Bunker survey & sampling coordination
  • SPRO issuance & de-mucking/de-slopping

2. Protective Agency (OPA)

In scenarios with potential conflicts (e.g., charterer-appointed agents), we serve as your independent protective layer:

  • Independent monitoring of port operations & delays
  • Verification & audit of disbursement accounts
  • Protection against overcharging or unfair practices
  • Detailed reporting & recommendations to owners
  • Full husbandry support as needed

3. Full Port Agency & Dry Docking Services

We handle complete port calls with efficiency and precision, backed by excellent shipyard relationships:

  • Berthing, pilotage, towage coordination
  • All documentation & authorities liaison
  • Dry docking supervision: scheduling, cost control, delay minimization
  • Standardized procedures to meet your timelines

4. Sale & Purchase (S&P) Handover Agency

Acting as the bridge between buyer and seller:

  • Coordination of vessel delivery formalities
  • Crew change & spares delivery & landing
  • Underwater inspections (UWI) & condition surveys
  • Smooth transition with minimal downtime

5. Newbuilding Vessel Agency

For vessels under construction in Chinese yards:

  • Liaison between yard, owner, and port authorities
  • Timely delivery coordination
  • Cost & delay minimization through strong networks
